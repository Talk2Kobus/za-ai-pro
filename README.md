\# ZA AI Pro



\*\*Your personal AI assistant — built in South Africa.\*\*



\[Open the Live App](https://za-ai-pro.streamlit.app)  

\*Ask questions in English or Afrikaans. Get answers. Save as PDF.\*



---



\## What It Does



\- Type a question → get a clear reply  

\- Works in \*\*English\*\* and \*\*Afrikaans\*\*  

\- Click \*\*"PDF"\*\* → download a professional report  

\- All chats saved in \*\*history\*\* (on the side)  

\- Shows \*\*South African time\*\* and your name

Go to: \[https://za-ai-pro.streamlit.app](https://za-ai-pro.streamlit.app)
